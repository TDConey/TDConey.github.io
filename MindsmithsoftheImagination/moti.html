About our site


I would like to welcome to the Mindsmiths of the Imagination site. One of the main goals of our site is to provide new writers with information by posting articles on how to improve they can improve their writing skills.

Another goal of this site is to inform writers on how to copyright their works, the facts and the fictions surrounding copyright laws on literature. Even through writing, at times can be frustrating, remember many of the great writers like those that wrote the books in the slideshow above had to start somewhere.

We have also posted helpful tools such as books the might prove helpful, not to mention links to other writing sites, and posted links to sites that are holding fiction writing contests.

In the future, we will offer an interactive forum where our writers can help one another or even collaborate on projects that will be published on our site.
