Slowly Dominic sat himself up on the couch and began to wipe the sleep from his eyes, as he glanced at Eric with a confused look on his face. As Dominic moved his head, Eric was alarmed to see a very large bruise that was along the side of his friends face., so he asked him how he was feeling. Dominic made a dismissive gesture with his hand saying that this is just a flesh wound and a true knight like himself could endure this and worse easily. Worried that his friend's injury wasn't the only injury he had sustained, Eric asked him his name only to have Dominic looked at him as if he were insane! He stood straight up and declared that he was none other than the famous Sir Gawain of New Rapport! Defender of the code of Chivalry, the weak, and true justice! Eric asked him how he know that he was a knight only to have Sir Gawain place his right hand over his chest and tell him because his heart told him that it is so! He thanked Eric for being such a gracious host for providing him shelter in his castle but he must get his valiant steed Rocinate ready to continue their quest. Sir Gawain asks Eric if he knows of a good blacksmith where he could get another helm to replace the one that he had lost in a battle a couple of days ago. Curiously Eric asks him what happened to his last helm, only to be told by Sir Gawain that he lost it in a battle against some brigands who had captured a fellow knight! Eric lifted something up with his left hand and gave it to Sir Gawain, as he then asks him if this belonged to him.
